General:

  1. The Tangerine Bank (the “Bank”) Savings Interest Offer of 2.50% (the “Offer”) is available to all new and existing Clients between April 8, 2014 and June 30, 2014 (the “Offer Period”) on all net new deposits made between April 8, 2014 and June 30, 2014 (the “Promotion Period”) to a maximum of $250,000.00. The Offer is applicable to net new deposits made to a Tangerine Savings Account (“Savings”), Tangerine Tax-Free Savings Accounts (“TFSA Savings”), Tangerine Retirement Savings Accounts (“RSP Savings”) and Tangerine Retirement Income Fund Savings Accounts (“RIF Savings”) [hereinafter collectively referred to as “Applicable Accounts”].

  2. During the Offer Period, the Bank will calculate Additional Interest on eligible net new deposits for existing Applicable Accounts each time the account balance exceeds the April 7, 2014 aggregate balance. For Applicable Accounts created during the Promotion Period by new Clients or existing Clients without an Applicable Account, the Bank will calculate Additional Interest on eligible daily closing balances for the duration of the Offer.

  3. All existing deposit balances in Applicable Accounts prior to the Promotion Period are not eligible for Additional Interest and will be paid the Bank’s standard posted rate of interest.

Additional Interest:

  1. “Additional Interest” shall be the difference between 2.50% and the Bank’s standard posted rate of interest payable on Applicable Accounts. For example, if on April 8, 2014, the Bank’s posted rate of interest on Applicable Accounts is 1.30%, the difference between the posted rate and 2.50% is 1.20%, therefore the Additional Interest rate payable on that date would be 1.20% (2.50% - 1.30% = 1.20%).

    Additional Interest Earned Daily = Net New Deposit Dollars X (0.0120 ÷ 365)

  2. Subject to the limits and qualifications more fully contained in these Rules and Regulations, Additional Interest shall be calculated daily and payable monthly on net new deposits made during the Promotion Period into Applicable Accounts which were created prior to this Offer. Additional Interest shall be calculated daily and payable monthly based on the eligible daily closing balances of Applicable Accounts created during the Promotion Period.

    Net new deposits to TFSA Savings and RSP Savings Accounts which were created before or during the Promotion Period are subject to the limits imposed by the Canada Revenue Agency (the “CRA”). The TFSA contribution limit for 2014 is $5,500 and the total contribution amount permitted across all TFSA accounts opened with any financial institution since the product was introduced in 2009 is $31,000. The RSP contribution limit is as noted on Client’s 2013 Notice of Assessment issued by the CRA. The Client is fully responsible for monitoring their individual contribution limits and ensuring any and all deposits fall within these set CRA limits. Client further agrees to at all times indemnify and hold the Bank harmless in respect of any such penalties or charges imposed upon the Client as a result of these imposed limits.

  3. Subject to the imposed CRA limits on TFSA and RSP accounts, Additional Interest shall be paid on the first $250,000.00 of net new deposit dollars made to each of the Applicable Accounts. No net new deposits or eligible daily balances to which Additional Interest would otherwise qualify shall be greater than $250,000.00 across each of the Applicable Accounts. Any balances greater than $250,000.00 will earn interest at the standard posted rate of interest payable on such Applicable Account.

    Example: Total Account balance: $350,000.00

    Net New Deposits: $300,000

    CAP ($250,000.00): $250,000.00 @ 1.20% rate of interest per annum

    Remainder: ($350,000 - $300,000) + ($300,000.00 – $250,000.00) @ posted rate of interest per annum

  4. The following transactions will be considered to be net new money and will be eligible for Additional Interest:

    1. Money transferred from a Savings Account into a RSP Savings or TFSA Savings Account.

    2. Money transferred from Tangerine Chequing to a Savings, RSP Savings, or TFSA Savings Account.

    3. New contributions from another financial institution including the Client’s linked account, to an eligible Savings, RSP Savings or TFSA Savings Account.

    4. Money transferred from another financial institution to an eligible RSP Savings, RIF Savings or TFSA Savings.

    5. Money transferred from a Children’s Savings Account (“CSA”) to a parent’s TFSA Savings and RSP Savings.

  5. Notwithstanding the above, the following is not considered to be net new money and will not be eligible for Additional Interest:

    1. Transfers made to any new RSP Savings from an existing RSP Savings or RSP GIC.

    2. Money deposited to a RSP Savings and then transferred into a RSP GIC resulting in no change to the aggregate balance of the RSP Savings from April 7, 2014.

    3. Transfers made to any new TFSA Savings from an existing TFSA Savings or TFSA GIC.

    4. Money deposited to a TFSA Savings and then transferred to a TFSA GIC resulting in no change to the aggregate balance of the TFSA Savings from April 7, 2014.

    5. Money transferred from a CSA to the parent’s Savings.

  6. Additional Interest will not be paid on direct or indirect transfers from Tangerine Business Savings Accounts and Tangerine US$ Business Savings Accounts.

  7. Additional Interest will not be paid on transfers to external accounts that are subsequently deposited back into one or more Applicable Accounts unless such deposits increase the aggregate balance in the respective Applicable Account above the aggregate balance as at April 7, 2014. In such case, only the portion above the April 7, 2014 aggregate balance may be eligible for Additional Interest.

  8. Additional Interest shall not be paid and shall not be payable until the start of the month following the month in which it was calculated (Example: If the month ends on April 30th 2014, the Bank’s standard posted rate of interest will be paid to that Applicable Account on April 30th 2014 and any Additional Interest will be paid to such Account on May 1st 2014). Any Applicable Account closed prior to the payment of Additional Interest will forfeit any Additional Interest calculated from the date that Applicable Account is closed.

  9. All interest payments are ineligible for the purposes of calculating Additional Interest and will not be calculated for the purposes of determining eligible daily balances. In other words, any interest paid into an Applicable Account, including Additional Interest, will not be treated as a new deposit for subsequently calculating Additional Interest payments.

Eligibility:

  1. Applicable Accounts created prior to April 8, 2014, are eligible for Additional Interest on the portion of the account balance that exceeds the closing balance as at April 7, 2014.

  2. New Clients, and existing Bank Clients with no current Applicable Accounts, are eligible to participate in this Offer should they open an Applicable Account during the Promotion Period. Under these circumstances, an Applicable Account’s total daily account balance will qualify for Additional Interest, excluding any interest payments made thereto.

  3. Existing Bank Clients who have an open Applicable Account prior to April 8, 2014, may open a new Savings, TFSA Savings, RSP Savings or RIF Savings account, however, only balances that exceed their aggregate April 7, 2014 balance will be eligible for the Additional Interest.

  4. Example 1: Mr. /Mrs. Client has $10,000 in his/her existing RSP Savings on April 7, 2014. On April 10, 2014, he/she deposits $5,000 to a new RSP Savings from their Tangerine Savings Account. They also transfer $5,000 from his/her existing RSP Savings to a RSP GIC. His/her aggregate balance in their RSP Savings remains $10,000 after completing the transfer. Therefore the deposit to the new RSP Savings is not eligible for the Additional Interest.

    Example 2: Mr. /Mrs. Client has $10,000 in his/her existing RSP Savings on April 7, 2014. On April 10, 2014, he/she deposits $10,000 to their existing RSP Savings from their Tangerine Savings Account. They also transfer $5,000 from his/her existing RSP Savings to a RSP GIC. His/her aggregate balance in their RSP Savings is $15,000 after completing the transfer. Therefore only $5,000 of the new deposit to the existing RSP Savings is eligible for the Additional Interest.

    Example 3: Mr. /Mrs. Client has $10,000 in his/her existing RSP Savings on April 7, 2014. On April 10, 2014, he/she opens an additional RSP Savings and transfers $5,000 from his/her existing RSP Savings. His/her aggregate balance remains $10,000 across all RSP Savings Accounts. Therefore the transfer to the new RSP Savings is not eligible for the Additional Interest because it does not qualify as a new deposit.
